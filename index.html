    Information

    SMPL HearSay can record the microphone while playing a simulated farend talker over the loudspeaker

    The recordings can be saved locally and then "shared" (eg by emailing the saved .wav file)

    Currently the recordings cannot be uploaded to a server (yet...)

    Some devices such as desktops and laptops have 2 or more output devices (eg built-in loudspeakers AND headphone outputs)

    On these multi-output devices, a simulated local talker can also be played (eg by connecting the headphone output to a separate external loudspeaker or artificial mouth)

    Unfortunately, even though many cellphones have built-in loudspeakers and headphone outputs, they typically have only a single D/A converter and cannot play different files over separate outputs
